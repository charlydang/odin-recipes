Baked Salmon

Salmon is a mild-flavored fish with a rich and slightly oily taste to it. Baked Salmon will often taste citrusy and buttery, along with whatever accompanying flavors were added to the dish.

Ingrediants

  • 12 ounce salmon fillet, cut into 4 pieces
  • Coarse-grained salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Toasted Almond Parsley Salsa, for serving
  • Baked squash, for serving, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon, skin side down, on a non-stick baking sheet or in a non-stick pan with an oven-proof handle.
  4. Bake until salmon is cooked through, about 12 to 15 minutes.
